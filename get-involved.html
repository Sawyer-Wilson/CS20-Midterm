Get Involved

Become A Volunteer!

Volunteers are the heart of Park St. Produce and are the key to our success. As a community centered farmers market, we rely on the support from locals to staff many events and aid in the setting and cleaning up of our weekly market. As a volunteer, you get early access and priority when signing up for our special events. You can register to volunteer for an upcoming event by filling out this form.

Donate!

You can make a big difference in your local community through a donation! Donations can be made electronically using either Venmo or Paypal (@ParkStProduce) or by mailing a check to our offices located at 75 State St, Boston, MA 02109. For information regarding claiming a donation as tax deductible, contact fundraising@parkstproduce.com

Become A Vender!

Park St. Produce has a wide range of venders but we are always looking for more local businesses to promote! For more information on leasing a space at Park St. Produce contact support@parkstproduce.com

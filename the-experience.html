We partner with vendors from all corners of Massachusetts! Everyone who participates is extremely excited to share their passion, craft, and products with you. On a typical day, you will find fresh seasonal produce (fruits, vegetables, and plants) from a variety of local farms, expertly baked goods from professional artisans, a prime meat selection from local butchers, a variety of delicious deserts from local bakeries, and much more. Go check out the vendors page to see which vendors will be participating on a given week.

Park St. Produce also honors local artists! We partner with bands and solo artists every other week to provide you with live music as you explore the market. Be sure to stop by and show your appreciation for their music; they always enjoy a crowd!

Park St. Produce is child and pet friendly! Feel free to bring your children and pets along for the ride. We have play areas for the kids and plenty of space for you and your entire family to explore.

Community Oriented ü´Ç

You will also notice that Park St. Produce is extremely community oriented -for it is the community that fuels Park St. Produce. In addition to the farmers market itself, we typically have group activities for you and your friends and family to enjoy. Whether you decide to play cornhole, mega chess, or take pictures, we hope that you say hi to your neighbors!
